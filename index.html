PRKR

At ConUHacks IV, Canadian Heritage challenged the participants to make an app that helps the English community in Quebec. Users of prkr will take a picture of any french parking sign, and the application will tell you if you can park there, and for how long, and when the parking spot will be available. The mobile frontend is made with Flutter, and the Python backend runs on Google App Engine. The backend sends the image to Google Cloud Vision to be analyzed. The application also uses data to present fun facts about parking in the city.

Flutter, Python, Google Cloud Vision (ConUHacks 2019)
